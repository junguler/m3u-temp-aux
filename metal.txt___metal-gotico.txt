Gothic metal is a subgenre of heavy metal that incorporates elements of gothic rock, darkwave, and classical music. It is characterized by its dark, melancholic sound, often featuring haunting vocals, symphonic orchestration, and heavy guitar riffs. The lyrics often deal with themes of death, despair, and the supernatural. Bands in this genre often wear elaborate costumes and makeup, and their live performances are known for their theatricality.
