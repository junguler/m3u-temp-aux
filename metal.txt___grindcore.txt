Grindcore is a subgenre of extreme metal that is characterized by its aggressive and abrasive sound. It typically features fast-paced, heavily distorted guitars, blast beat drumming, and guttural vocals. The lyrics often focus on social and political issues, as well as themes of violence and gore. Grindcore bands are known for their short, intense songs that rarely exceed two minutes in length.
