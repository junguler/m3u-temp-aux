Eurotrance is a high-energy subgenre of trance music that emerged in the late 1990s, characterized by its infectious melodic hooks, driving basslines, and uplifting rhythms. It blends elements of techno and house with soaring synth leads, often incorporating anthemic build-ups and euphoric breakdowns. Eurotrance is known for its capacity to create a euphoric atmosphere on dance floors, combining emotional melodies with a fast-paced, danceable beat typically ranging between 130 to 150 BPM.
