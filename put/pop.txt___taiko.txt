Taiko music is a traditional Japanese percussion genre that features large drums called taiko. The music is characterized by powerful, rhythmic beats that are often accompanied by chanting or other vocalizations. Taiko music is commonly used in festivals and other cultural events in Japan, and has gained popularity around the world in recent years.
