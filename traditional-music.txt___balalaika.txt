Balalaika music is a traditional Russian folk genre that features the use of the balalaika, a triangular-shaped stringed instrument. The music is characterized by its lively and upbeat tempo, with intricate melodies and harmonies that are often accompanied by other traditional Russian instruments such as the domra and the accordion.
