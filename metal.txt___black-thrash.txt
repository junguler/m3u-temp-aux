Black thrash is a subgenre of heavy metal that combines the aggressive and fast-paced elements of thrash with the dark and sinister themes of black metal. It is characterized by its raw and primitive sound, often featuring harsh vocals, distorted guitars, and pounding drums. The lyrics often focus on topics such as Satanism, violence, and anti-Christianity. Black thrash bands often incorporate elements of punk and hardcore into their music, creating a unique and intense sound. Some of the pioneers of the genre include Sodom, Bathory, and Venom.
