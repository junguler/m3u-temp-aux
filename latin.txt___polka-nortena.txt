Polka nortena is a lively and upbeat music genre that originated in northern Mexico and Texas. It is characterized by the use of accordion and brass instruments, and often features lyrics about love, life, and the struggles of the working class. The music is typically played at social gatherings and dances, and is enjoyed by people of all ages. Some popular sub-genres of polka nortena include mariachi polkas and conjunto polkas.
