Christian punk is a subgenre of punk rock that incorporates Christian themes and values into the lyrics. The music is characterized by fast-paced guitar riffs, heavy drum beats, and aggressive vocals. The lyrics often focus on faith, hope, and redemption, and encourage listeners to live a life that reflects their beliefs.
