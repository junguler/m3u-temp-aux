Neo-trad doom metal is a subgenre of doom metal that combines traditional elements of the genre with modern influences. It is characterized by heavy, slow and melancholic riffs, dark lyrics and a strong emphasis on atmosphere. The music often features clean vocals and epic, symphonic arrangements. Bands in this genre often draw inspiration from classic doom metal bands such as Black Sabbath and Candlemass, but also incorporate elements of progressive rock, post-metal and other genres.
