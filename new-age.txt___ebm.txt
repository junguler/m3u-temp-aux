EBM, or Electronic Body Music, is a subgenre of industrial music that emerged in the early 1980s. It is characterized by its use of synthesizers, drum machines, and distorted vocals, creating a hard-hitting and danceable sound. EBM often incorporates elements of punk, new wave, and techno, and is known for its aggressive and energetic style.
