Rumba is a lively music genre that originated in Cuba and has since spread throughout Latin America and Spain. It is characterized by its upbeat tempo and use of percussion instruments such as the conga and bongo drums. Rumba often incorporates elements of flamenco and salsa music, and is known for its catchy melodies and danceable rhythms.
