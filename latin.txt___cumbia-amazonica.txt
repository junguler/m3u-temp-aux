Cumbia Amazonica is a music genre that originated in the Amazon region of Peru. It is characterized by its upbeat tempo, use of traditional instruments such as the charango and accordion, and incorporation of indigenous rhythms and melodies. The lyrics often focus on themes of love, nature, and the Amazonian way of life.
