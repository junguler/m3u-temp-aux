Vocal house music is a subgenre of house music that incorporates soulful vocals and catchy melodies. It typically features a four-on-the-floor beat and uplifting lyrics that encourage dancing and positive vibes. The genre emerged in the 1980s and has since evolved to include elements of pop, R&B, and electronic dance music. Some of the defining characteristics of vocal house music include its use of piano chords, gospel-inspired vocals, and a focus on creating a sense of community and togetherness on the dance floor.
