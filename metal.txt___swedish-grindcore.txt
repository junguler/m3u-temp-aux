Swedish grindcore is a subgenre of extreme metal that combines elements of punk and death metal. It is characterized by its fast and aggressive sound, with heavily distorted guitars, blast beats, and guttural vocals. The lyrics often deal with social and political issues, and the music is known for its raw and intense energy. Bands in this genre typically have short, fast-paced songs that are meant to be played at high volume. Some of the most notable bands in this genre include Nasum, Gadget, and Regurgitate.
