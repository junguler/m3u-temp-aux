Cali rap is a subgenre of hip hop that originated in California and is characterized by its heavy use of bass and synthesizers. The lyrics often focus on street life, gang culture, and the struggles of living in urban areas. The beats are often upbeat and energetic, with catchy hooks and repetitive choruses.
